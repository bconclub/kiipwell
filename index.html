Who We Are

A Home Built on Trust
& Lived Experience

We are a supported housing provider dedicated to working with young people aged 16–21, including asylum seekers and refugees.

Our team brings together experience in housing, safeguarding, and youth support, alongside lived experience of the asylum journey.

We are committed to supporting young people to grow, develop independence, and build a positive future.

Our Approach

Not Just to Survive,
But to Thrive

We work alongside young people to help them prepare for independence. Each young person receives tailored support based on their needs, goals, and lived experiences.
